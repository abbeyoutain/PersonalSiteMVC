About Me

I'm Abbey Outain, a full stack web developer.

My background is in English; I’ve been a lifelong reader and writer, but when looking for the next steps in my career, I wanted something that involved more logic and problem solving than putting pen to paper.

I attended Centriq Training’s Full Stack Web Developer program, and not only did I fall in love with coding, I also found the creative and critical thinking skills relevant to writing were essential and significant to being a developer. I think my portfolio shows my passion for both the creative and logical aspects of developing, particularly my C# text-based mini-game where you rid a fey castle monsters.
